Interdisciplinary Exchange for the Americas

Our Vision

Innovation can come from anywhere. By providing access, expertise and resources, our program will foster collaboration in the Americas, starting with Cuba. Our goal is to build entrepreneurial momentum in the digital space and to increase access to STEM in local communities.

Why Cuba

Cuba - strong technical expertise despite connectivity issues. Academic institutions produce top talent. Entrepreneurial spirit Cuban labor force are increasingly becoming self-employed cuentapropistas. Growing awareness of opportunities in technology. Existing initiatives around STEM (ex: Project Sigma)
